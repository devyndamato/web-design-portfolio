Who I Am

I am a current senior at the University of Denver, majoring in Strategic Communications with a Marketing minor. Throughout my time in school and through my internship experience, I have developed a strong passion for public relations, from building media relationships to crafting compelling brand stories. As I prepare to graduate, I am excited to continue growing in the PR industry and to contribute to campaigns that make an impact.

I was born in Denver but grew up in Overland Park, Kansas, a suburb of Kansas City. Some of my favorite things about Kansas City are the amazing barbecue, little to no traffic, home-cooked meals from my parents, and how green everything is.

Outside of my professional life, I enjoy traveling, spending time outdoors, reading, and attending live music. I love finding new places to explore, whether that is in a new city or somewhere close to home. Getting outside and being active helps me reset, and reading is one of my favorite ways to unwind.
