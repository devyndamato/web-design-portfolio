# Devyn D'Amato Portfolio Website

This website is my professional portfolio created for MFJS 3501 – Web Design & Content Development at the University of Denver. The site highlights my academic work, internship experience, and projects related to strategic communications, public relations, and marketing.

## Project Purpose

The goal of this website is to present my professional background and portfolio in a clear, organized, and visually consistent format. It is designed for potential employers, collaborators, and instructors who want to quickly understand my experience and see examples of my work.

## Site Pages

- **Home** – Overview of my portfolio and introduction to my work
- **About** – Background, education, and professional interests
- **Portfolio** – Internship experiences, projects, and an interactive timeline
- **Contact** – Ways to connect with me, including a contact form and LinkedIn
- **Showcase** – A project case study explaining the concept, process, and tools used to build this website
